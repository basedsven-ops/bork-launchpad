Introduction

BORK is a hybrid tokenized-stock launchpad deployed on Robinhood Chain L2 (Arbitrum Orbit Stack).

By pairing the virality of memecoins with the economic stability of Real-World Assets (RWA), BORK allows creators to deploy permissionless tokens backed natively by fractionalized equities and ETFs such as Reddit ($RDDT), NVIDIA ($NVDA), and Tesla ($TSLA).

Key Innovation: Unlike typical launchpads that rely purely on volatile native currencies (like ETH) or virtual liquidity, BORK assets are anchored to real on-chain equities.

How it Works

Deploying and trading stock-backed memecoins on BORK is completed in three trustless phases:

1

Launch with Stock Backing

Creators specify their token's name, symbol, target collateral, and the backing RWA equity token. The factory mints 1,000,000,000 memecoins and holds them securely.

2

Bonding Curve Phase

Traders buy meme tokens directly using the specified RWA stock token. 80% of the total meme supply is distributed dynamically based on a linear funding rate.

3

Direct V3 Pool Locking

Once the RWA collateral reaches 100% of the target (e.g. 10 RDDT), the factory deposits the remaining 20% of the memecoins and 100% of collected RWA tokens directly into a Uniswap V3 Pool (1% fee tier) and initializes the price ratio, locking the liquidity forever.

Hybrid Bonding Curve & Graduation valuation

Unlike standard bonding curves where creators can set arbitrary funding targets, BORK implements a fixed $69,000 USD Market Cap graduation target (matching standard pump.fun specifications). This guarantees a standard initial valuation for all memecoins graduating to Uniswap V3.

The bonding curve completed state requires raising exactly 20% of the graduation market cap ($13,800 USD) in RWA collateral tokens. This is calculated dynamically based on the RWA price:

\[\text{Target Collateral Needed} = \frac{\$13,800 \text{ USD}}{\text{Market Price of RWA Token}}\]

Example Target Computations:

  • Reddit ($RDDT) at $62.45: requires 220.98 RDDT target collateral.
  • NVIDIA ($NVDA) at $125.00: requires 110.40 NVDA target collateral.
  • Tesla ($TSLA) at $220.50: requires 62.59 TSLA target collateral.
  • Wrapped Ethereum ($WETH) at $3,450.00: requires 4.00 WETH target collateral.

Uniswap V3 Integration

BORK implements a zero-migration, single-sided liquidity listing model on Uniswap V3:

  • Tick Spacing: Standardized at 200 ticks (for the 1% fee tier).
  • Range: Full range liquidity placement from tick -887200 to 887200.
  • Safe price initialization: The factory computes the exact square-root price ratio mathematically:
\[\text{sqrtPriceX96} = \sqrt{\frac{\text{Collateral LP Amount}}{\text{Meme LP Amount}}} \times 2^{96}\]

Using the custom uniswapV3MintCallback interface, the pool pulls tokens securely during initialization. The ownership of the Uniswap V3 position NFT is permanently burned (sent to address(0) or the creator to be locked) ensuring the pool can never be rugged.

Creator Incentives

To incentivize builders to launch proper, high-quality projects, BORK leverages Uniswap V3's native fee-collection mechanism:

  1. Since the V3 liquidity position belongs to the creator (or is locked in a vault), all trading fees generated in the 1% fee tier pool are dynamically tracked.
  2. Token creators can claim these generated RWA fees directly on-chain, creating a sustainable revenue stream based on trading volume.

Auditing & Safety

Security Auditing: The BORK factory smart contracts are fully checked with the Foundry test suite under popping poptye RPC forks.

Key safety measures implemented:

  • No migration risks: Unlike traditional curve systems, there are no temporary contracts storing massive assets. Liquidity goes directly to standard Uniswap V3.
  • Standard ERC-20 integration: All whitelist RWA tokens are treated as standard ERC-20s, requiring active allowances for all transfers.
